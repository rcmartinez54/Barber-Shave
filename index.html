Meet our team

Nellie Blunt PA-C

(Physician Assistant – Certified) Graduated from Stanford University Physician Assistant Program in 2004, she is board certified and continues her on going education in this field. She has worked for Merz and Galderma as an educator in cosmetic dermatology. She trains other providers (MD’s, NP’s, PA’s, RN’s) in their practices, as well as training the trainers from across the US. Nellie has over 10 years experience with injectables, IPL, and lasers. She holds cosmetic training certificates from prominent cosmetic companies and has extensive training in facial contouring and facial aesthetics. She prides herself on exceeding the standard of care and delivers effective and safe treatments with exceptional care all while maintaining the highest level of comfort.

Nellie has been performing the following procedures for over 10 years; Botox, Xeomin, Dysport, Radiesse, Belotero, Juvederm, Voluma, Restylane (Lyft, Silk, Refyne and Defyne), Sculptra, Cryotherapy, Kybella, Sclerotherapy, IPL (skin and hair), B12 and MIC-B shots. She has had extensive training in facial contouring and facial aesthetics, and holds cosmetic training certificates from prominent cosmetic companies including Allergan, Lumenis, Syneron, Palomar Cutera, Merz and NPI’s Sclerotherapy Course. Nellie continues privately teaching Physicians, Nurse Practitioners, Physician Assistants and Registered Nurses how to perform the above services in their practices.

Nellie exceeds the standard of care with on-going training to deliver effective and safe treatments. Our Medical Director Anshu Gupta MD and Nellie Blunt PA-C are dedicated to delivering exceptional care all while maintaining the highest level of comfort.
